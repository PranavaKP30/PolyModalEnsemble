EXPERIMENTAL SETUP
=========================

Dataset:
  Name: Amazon Reviews 5-Class Rating Prediction
  Classes: 5 (1★, 2★, 3★, 4★, 5★)
  Train samples: 500
  Test samples: 100

Evaluation:
  Cross-validation: 5-fold
  Random seed: 42
  Metrics: Accuracy, Star MAE, Close Accuracy

Models tested:
  - Baseline models: Random Forest, XGBoost, SVM, etc.
  - Ensemble methods: Voting, Bagging, Stacking
  - MainModel: Multimodal ensemble with dropout
